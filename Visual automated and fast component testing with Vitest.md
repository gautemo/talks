# Visual, automated, and fast component testing with Vitest
Format: Presentation 👨‍🏫

### 9 April 2026 - Gjensidige TechDay
### 24 April 2026 - CapraCon

## Description
What exactly is component testing, and where does it fit within the testing pyramid? In this talk, we explore how Vitest enables fast, visual, and reliable component testing—and why it matters. We’ll walk through practical examples, then compare Vitest with Cypress and Playwright.
